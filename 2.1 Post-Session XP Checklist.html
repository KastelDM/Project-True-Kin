Post-session XP Checklist

At the end of a game session, reflect on your experiences and check the criteria boxes based on them. You receive 1 XP per checked box. A single event can only count towards a single checkbox.

Discovery

Reached a new place (settlement, landmark, vault or point of interest).

Unearthed a secret or hidden truth.

Gained rare knowledge or exotica.

Intrigue

Made an impression or impact on an important faction, community, individual or political system.

Made a significant friend or foe.

Completed a major quest or journey.

Peril

Resolved or navigated a meaningful conflict (peacefully or otherwise).

Tasted a meaningful defeat or victory.

Suffered a critical wound.

Character

Growth or transformation.

Roleplayed a character trait to a fault.

Acquired a new character feature, such as a Talent or Cybernetic Implant.

Bonus XP

The GM may choose to bestow you 1-3 bonus XP based on improvised criteria, such as accompishing an impressive feat.
