Character Creation

Quick Start Overview

  1. Print a character sheet
  2. Choose an Ancestry and name your character
  3. Roll Abilities
  4. Roll Background & Personality
  5. Choose a Mystic Gift or Cybernetic Implant
  6. Roll HP and calculate Burdens
  7. Roll Starting Gear and Exotica
  8. Finish details and send your character to the referee

1. Get a Character Sheet

  1. Download and print a Blank Character Sheet.
  2. For reference, here is a completed example.
  3. Read the one-page setting intro: What is Vaarn?, then return to these instructions.

2. Choose an Ancestry & Name Your Character

Your Ancestry defines what kind of being you are, what languages you speak, and any special features you begin play with. Choose one Ancestry from the table below, then give your character a name.

Each Ancestry has a linked page with tables for names and appearance. These are optional, but recommended if you want inspiration.

Ancestry Languages Known Special
True-Kin (human) Vaarnish, Hegemonic Standard Pure of Blood – Advantage on reaction rolls with other true-kin (lost if visibly mutated).
Inheritor – Advantage on checks to identify and use pre-collapse technology.
Cacogen (mutant) Vaarnish Corrupted Blood – Roll 1–3 mutations during character creation.
Synth (robot or android) Vaarnish, LogLang Syntax Synthetic Flesh – No need to eat or breathe; immune to biological hazards; take double damage from electricity.
Synthetic Mind – Vulnerable to hacking and malicious LogLang code.
Newbeast (sapient animal) Vaarnish Beasthood – Advantage or disadvantage where your animal nature meaningfully helps or hinders.
Mycomorph (corpse-fungus hybrid) Vaarnish Twice Born – Attempt to recall knowledge from your original body.
Detritivore – Consume rotting organic matter; double healing from Short Rests if your meal is decayed.

3. Roll Your Abilities

You have six core Abilities:

  • Strength (STR) – Melee attacks, physical power, readied items
  • Dexterity (DEX) – Ranged attacks, speed, finesse
  • Constitution (CON) – Health recovery, endurance, carrying capacity
  • Psyche (PSY) – Perception, intuition, ammo conservation
  • Intellect (INT) – Knowledge, searching, languages, technology
  • Ego (EGO) – Morale, persuasion, charm, deception
Important: For each Ability, roll 3d6 and record the lowest die as the Ability’s value.

Example: You roll 2, 2, and 6 for Strength. The lowest die is 2, so your STR is 2.

4. Roll Background & Personality

  1. Use the generator below to roll a personality and background combo. If the result feels silly or unfun, just re-roll it!
  2. Record this info on your character sheet.

What does this effect?

Your background informs how NPCs react to you, what skills and knowledge you plausibly possess, and how you roleplay your character in Vaarn.

5. Choose a Mystic Gift or Cybernetic Implant

Choose one of the following and add it to your Features list:

  • Mystic Gift
  • Cybernetic Implant

You may only ever have one Cybernetic Implant installed per Ability.

6. Hit Points & Carrying Capacity

  • Hit Points: Roll 1d8. You may reroll once, but must keep the second result.
  • Maximum Burden Slots: CON + 10

Mark unusable Burden Slots with an X on your sheet.

7. Starting Gear & Burdens

  1. Add 5 rations of water (they stack in one Burden Slot).
  2. If you chose a Mystic Gift, it occupies 1 Burden Slot.
  3. Roll once on each of the Starting Gear Tables.
  4. Roll once on the Exotica table.
Burden Slot States:
[ ] Carried – requires an action to retrieve
[R] Readied – usable instantly (Up to STR + 2 slots)
[W] Worn – armor, clothing, Mystic Gifts
[X] Unusable – exceeds your max burden

If any items exceed your maximum Burden Slots, discard items until all X’d slots are empty.

8. Final Details

  • Level: 1
  • XP: 0 (10 XP to reach level 2)
  • Readied Items: STR + 2 Burden Slots

9. You’re Ready to Play

Send your referee either a copy of your character sheet or the following details:

  • Name
  • Ancestry
  • Background & Personality
  • Mystic Gift or Cybernetic
  • HP and Armor Value
