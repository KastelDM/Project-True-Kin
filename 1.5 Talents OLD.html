Talents

d20 Talent Description
1 Brawler When you roll damage for any improvised or unarmed attack, rolls of 3 or lower are treated as if they were 4's.
2 Bullet Bender You ignore the optimal range for (almost) all weapons and are able to make attacks up to Long range without incurring disadvantage on the Attack Roll.
3 Biokinetic You can spend spend Hit Points to heal biological creatures and cure ailments.

Normal range: C

This Talent occupies a Burden Slot.
4 Commander The last target you attack on your turn is marked. The next ally to attack your marked target before the start of your next turn gains advantage on the Attack Roll.
5 Cryokinetic You can spend Hit Points to generate intense cold, freezing matter.

Normal Range: C-S

This Talent occupies a Burden Slot.
6 Defender Once per round, you can take the place of a close ally when they are subject to an attack, pushing them out harm's way and making yourself the target of the attack instead.

Normal Range: C
7 Nuisance When you attack a creature, It has disadvantage on the next attack it makes against a target that isn't you before the start of your next turn.
8 Relentless You can spend Hit Points to make an extra Attack of Opportunity against a target you have not attacked since the end of your last turn. The HP cost starts at d4 and goes up a step for every extra creature attacked before the start of your next turn.
9 Oracle As an action, you can spend Hit Points to roll a d20, known as a Precognition Die, and record the result. You can then use this result in place of one ability check made by yourself or a creature you can see. Unused Precognition Die vanish after a long rest. HP cost starts at d4 and goes up a die size for each unspent Precognition Die.

You can also spend Hit Points to consult your oracular abilities and gain a sense of what might happen in the future.

This Talent occupies a Burden Slot.
10 Projectionist While you are unconscious or undergoing a meditative trance, you can project your consciousness outside of your body by spending Hit Points. While projecting, you can perceive everything in the physical world as normal, but you cannot interact with it, and you can only be detected by psychic creatures or specialized equipment. The more time and distance between your projected self and your body, the more taxing projection becomes.

Normal Range: C-M

This Talent occupies a Burden Slot.
11 Pyrokinetic You can spend Hit Points to generate intense heat or flames

Normal range: C-S

This Talent occupies a Burden Slot.
12 Psychometric You can spend Hit Points to gain insight into the history of an object you can touch.

This Talent occupies a Burden Slot.
13 Phantom You can spend Hit Points to turn invisible. HP cost increases in stressful circumstances, and you may need to succeed checks in order to maintain invisibility.

This Talent occupies a Burden Slot.
14 Dervish You can move up to 1 extra square when moving on your turn, and you are immune to Attacks of Opportunity from creatures you attacked previously on your turn.
15 Chronurgist You can bend spacetime. You have +1 Armour vs attacks you can see coming, and you can move 1 extra square in grid-based combat. You age at eight-tenths the rate of other members of your ancestry.

This Talent occupies a Burden Slot.
16 Accurate Your melee and ranged attacks can crit on a 19.
17 Telekinetic You can spend Hit Points to move creatures or objects with your mind. Distance, mass, and acceleration are all factors that can make this ability more taxing.

You can also attempt to manipulate gravity and create force fields.

This Talent occupies a Burden Slot.
18 Telepath You can communicate telepathically with biological creatures and read their surface thoughts. Doing so with an unwilling creature costs Hit Points and has a chance to fail. Distance and barriers between yourself and the target can cause more strain.

Normal range: C-S

This Talent occupies a Burden Slot.
19 Tough You gain +2 Maximum Hit Points per level, and also gain +2 Maximum Hit Points whenever you level up.

Additionally, you can remain concious upon sustaining a Major Wound if it does not kill you.
20 Vibrokinetic You can spend Hit Points to produce precise sounds and vibrations at different frequencies and amplitudes. higher-amplitude effects are more taxing and dangerous to produce.

This Talent occupies a Burden Slot.
