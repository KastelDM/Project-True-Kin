Wounds

Minor Wounds

Confused

INT disadvantage. When you target an area or any creature involved in melee, roll a d6. On a 4–6, the action fails or hits an unintended target instead.

Distracted

PSY disadvantage. Talent potency rolls are made with disadvantage.

Shaken

EGO disadvantage. At the start of your turn, make an Ego save. On a failure, you can’t willingly approach danger until your next turn.

Sickened

CON disadvantage. Healing you receive is halved (and rounded down).

Slowed

DEX disadvantage. Your movement speed is halved.

Staggered

STR disadvantage. You can’t make Attacks of Opportunity.

Critical Wounds

Comatose

-d8 INT and -d8 STR. You fall into a coma for d100 days and require a caretaker to survive.

Destroyed Eye

-d8 EGO and -d8 PSY.

Destroyed Limb

Loss of use of the limb below the amputation point. Roll separately for permanent STR and DEX loss based on the level of the injury:

  • Wrist or ankle: d6
  • Elbow or knee: d8
  • Shoulder or hip: d12

Enduring Blindness

-d8 PSY. You are blind.

Enduring Deafness

-d8 PSY. You are deaf.

Enduring Silence

-d8 EGO. You lose the ability to speak.

False Reality

-d8 EGO and -d8 INT. You suffer a lasting delusion or false belief.

Fragmented Identity

-d8 EGO and -d8 INT. A disruptive personality fragment emerges.

Instilled Fear

-d8 EGO and -d8 INT. You develop an overwhelming fear of something specific.

Necrosis

-d8 CON. Also lose d8 DEX or d8 PSY depending on the affected extremity:

  • Fingers or toes: DEX
  • Ears or nose: PSY

Nerve Damage

-d8 DEX and -d8 PSY.

Ruined Appearance

-d8 EGO. You gain advantage on Intimidation due to your disfigurement.

Systemic Damage

-d8 maximum Hit Points and -d8 CON.

Veiled Memory

-d8 PSY and -d8 INT. You forget how to use one Language or Mystic Gift.

Withered Flesh

-d8 CON and -d8 DEX.

Your flesh decays, leaving you frail and wasted.

Special Wounds

Exhaustion

-1 Inventory Slot.
