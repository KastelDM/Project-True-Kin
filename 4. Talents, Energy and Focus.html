Talents, Energy, and Focus

Talents

Talents are special features that your character can possess.

  • At character creation, you may choose one free Talent.
  • Afterward, Talents are rarely earned through leveling up.
  • Most new Talents come through achievement and discovery. The GM may bestow Talents based on a characterâ€™s experiences, aptitudes, deeds or misdeeds.
  • There are two types of Talents: Energy Talents and Focus Talents. This distinction only matters when spending Energy Points (EP) or Focus Points (FP).

Energy Points and Focus Points

At character creation, and whenever you level up and spend Ability Points to increase Abilities, you also gain Energy Points and Focus Points.

EP and FP are finite resources. They do not replenish naturally.

Throughout your career, you may spend EP or FP to bend the rules in your favor.

Ways to Spend EP or FP

You can spend 1 EP or 1 FP to achieve any of the following effects:

  • Gain advantage on an Ability Check or To-hit roll.
    • EP: STR, DEX, or CON checks
    • FP: INT, PSY, or EGO checks
  • Ignore damage from using a Talent (up to d12 potency level).
    • EP: Energy Talent
    • FP: Focus Talent
  • Impose disadvantage on a Damage roll against you.
    • EP: Kinetic, Energy, or Toxic damage.
    • FP: Psychic damage.
