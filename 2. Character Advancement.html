Character Advancement

Gaining XP

To advance your character from level 1 to level 2, you must accumulate 10 XP. When you level up, your XP gets reset to 0, and you must accumulate 5 extra XP per level to level up again.

  • Level 1 -> 2: 10 XP
  • Level 2 -> 3: 15 XP
  • Level 3 -> 4: 20 XP
  • And so on.

Gaining XP is based on a post-session checklist system. At the end of a session, you can award your character XP according to criteria in the Post-Session XP Checklist. Each checked box awards 1 XP and can only be checked once per session. A single event can only count towards fulfilling one criterion. For example, if you get your arm hacked off, you can’t check both the “Critical Wound” and “Character Growth/Transformation” boxes. You may only check off one or the other.

Leveling up

When you level up your character, do the following:

  • Roll a d8. Your current and maximum hit points increase by the amount rolled.
  • You may reroll the result once, but you must stick with the new result if you do so.
  • Allocate 3 ability points to the ability bonuses of your choosing. Your ability defenses also increase accordingly.
  • Abilities can never be raised above +10/20.
  • For every ability point you spent in STR, DEX, or CON, give yourself an Energy Point.
  • For every ability point you spent in INT, PSY, or EGO, give yourself a Focus Point.
  • Roll a d10. On a 10, you can choose a Talent to add to your features list.
